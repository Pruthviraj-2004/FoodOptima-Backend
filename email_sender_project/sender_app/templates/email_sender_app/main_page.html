Project Overview

Streamlined Efficiency

FoodOptima is a web application designed to streamline meal planning in organizations. By gathering employee responses regarding their meal requirements for the next working day, FoodOptima ensures that meal orders align with actual demand, reducing waste and saving costs.

Problem & Solution

Many organizations struggle with over-ordering or under-ordering meals for employees, leading to food waste and increased expenses. Without a reliable system for gathering daily meal requirements, businesses often find themselves wasting resources or failing to meet demand.

Problem

FoodOptima automates the process of collecting meal preferences from employees for the upcoming workday. Responses are automatically saved, giving management an accurate count of how many meals to prepare or order, ensuring optimal meal planning.
